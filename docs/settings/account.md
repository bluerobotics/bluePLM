# Account Settings

Personal settings for your BluePLM account.

## Profile

Update your user profile:
- **Full Name** - How you appear to other users
- **Email** - Your sign-in email
- **Profile Picture** - Custom avatar that appears throughout the app

### Profile Picture

Your profile picture is displayed across BluePLM - in comments, file checkout indicators, team lists, and the navigation bar.

**Priority order:**
1. Custom uploaded picture (you can upload your own)
2. Google/OAuth picture (from your Google account if signed in with Google)
3. Initials (generated from your name/email)

**To upload a custom profile picture:**
1. Go to Settings → Profile
2. Hover over your current avatar
3. Click the camera icon
4. Select an image (PNG, JPG, or WebP, max 2MB)

**To remove your custom picture:**
1. Hover over your avatar
2. Click the X button that appears
3. Your avatar will revert to your Google picture or initials

## Preferences

Customize your experience:

### Language
Choose from supported languages:
- English
- Chinese (Simplified/Traditional)
- German
- French
- Spanish
- Portuguese

### Display
- **Theme** - Dark mode (light theme coming soon)
- **Sidebar mode** - Expanded, collapsed, or expand on hover

## Keybindings

Customize keyboard shortcuts for common actions:
- File operations (checkout, checkin, download)
- Navigation
- View toggles

Click a keybinding to record a new shortcut.

## Modules

Control which modules appear in the sidebar:
- Drag to reorder
- Toggle visibility
- Create custom groups
- Add dividers between sections

### Module Configuration
Each module can have:
- **Custom icon** - Choose from available icons
- **Custom color** - Change the icon color
- **Parent group** - Nest under another module

## Delete Account

Permanently delete your account and all associated data.

::: danger This cannot be undone
Deleting your account removes:
- Your user profile
- Your contributions
- Your settings

Files you've uploaded remain in the organization's vaults.
:::

To delete your account:
1. Go to Settings → Delete Account
2. Type your name to confirm
3. Click Delete Account
